About Me

I'm a Robotics enthusiast. I'm interested in solving technical problems related to Space Technologies, Human Disabilities and robotics in genaral.I'm pursuing Bechelors of Technology in Mechanical Engineering with Minor Specialization in Computer Engineering.

I have developed a state-of-the-art In-helmet spacesuit device which would work as a convenient extension of an astronaut during ExtraVehicular Activities(EVAs). More detail is available on the 'Projects' page. I had secured funding of Rs 40,000 from the Idea Lab, Nirma University to work on this project.

I'm also working on a Quadruped Robot under the guidance of Dr. Jatinkumar Dave. Robots like SPOTMINI and LAIKAGO inspired me a lot.

I was a research intern at Sea Tech Lab, Florida Atlantic University, FL, USA. I have been working on Path Planning and Obstacle Avoidance Algorithms for Unmanned Surface Vehicles(USVs) under the guidance of Dr. Manhar Dhanak and Armando Sinisterra. I did an Industrial Training from Tirth Agro. Technology Pvt. Ltd., Rajkot in my sophomore year. It was a great experience working with some of the highly experienced engineers. I have learned a lot about fast pace manufacturing, product designing and automation. I learned how to work in a stressful situation by receiving training from the world's largest manufacturer of rotary tillers.
