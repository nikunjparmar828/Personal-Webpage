About Me

My research is highly multidisciplinary in nature, combining elements from Engineering design, Computational engineering, Computer Programming, and Material Science. My work involves the design and development of Intelligent Robots.

Currently, I'm pursuing Bachelors of Technology in Mechanical Engineering with Minor Specialization in Computer Engineering at Institute of Technology, Nirma University, Ahmedabad, Gujarat, IN.

I recently got a chance to complete my final-year project at the Institute for Plasma Research (IPR), Gandhinagar. I was working on the Design and Prototype Development of a 3-DOF Parallel Haptic Arm. In the summer of 2019, I got an opportunity to work at Sea Tech Lab, Florida Atlantic University, FL, USA as a research scholar. I did research on optimal path planning of Unmanned Surface Vehicles in dynamic environments. I was guided by Dr. Manhar Dhanak and Armando Sinnisterra.

I’m also interested in Mechanical Product designing and Automation. In my sophomore year, I worked at Tirth Agro Technology Pvt. Ltd. as a Trainee. While working at the world’s largest Rotary Tiller manufacturer, I acquainted with technology in Engineering processes and related system besides techniques of manufacturing of Rotary Tillers and other Agri Implements with quality standards and the best practices of QMS & TPM.
